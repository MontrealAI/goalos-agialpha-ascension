HUMAN REVIEW CHECKLIST

What the reviewer must decide.

The node can organize evidence and surface a recommendation. The reviewer still owns the consequential judgment.

01

Identity

Is the declared node, operator, reviewer, jurisdiction, and lineage appropriate for this mission?

02

Mission

Are the success criteria, constraints, risk profile, and stop rules complete enough to govern the work?

03

Route

Were the chosen peers sufficiently capable, independent, diverse, and resilient?

04

Evidence

Do the artifacts support the claims, or merely document that a process occurred?

05

Dissent

Do validator objections reveal an unresolved contradiction, missing evidence, or unacceptable uncertainty?

06

Authority

What action, if any, should a human now authorize—and under what monitoring, rollback, and liability conditions?
