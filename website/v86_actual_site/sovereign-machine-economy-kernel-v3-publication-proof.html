Claim boundary

What this edition establishes—and what it does not.

The publication defines a theoretical systems and protocol architecture, an executable browser-local reference implementation, and a falsifiable evaluation program. The use cases and case studies are illustrative reference scenarios.

Not claimed:

  • achieved AGI, ASI, or superintelligence
  • empirical state of the art
  • independent certification or external audit completion
  • legal authority or regulatory approval
  • guaranteed ROI or economic return
  • production authorization or public production reliance
  • live settlement, token movement, or user-fund authorization
  • autonomous sovereignty, energy abundance, or civilization-scale capability
