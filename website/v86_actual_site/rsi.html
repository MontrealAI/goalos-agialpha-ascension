Proof Gradient control plane

RSI means proof-backed upgrade rights.

Recursive Self-Improvement is treated as a governance route: an artifact may influence future work only after evidence, evaluation, review, challenge, canary, monitoring, and rollback readiness. It does not claim achieved AGI, achieved ASI, or superintelligence achieved.
