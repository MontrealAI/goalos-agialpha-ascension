Quintessential use cases

Evidence before reliance.

Filter the institutional patterns by operating domain.

UC-01Strategy

Executive decision intelligence

Move consequential strategy from narrative to a source-bound, scenario-tested governed decision state.

Evidence burden
Primary-source room, scenario matrix, contradiction register, option portfolio, risk ledger.
Authority boundary
Executive owner records disposition; no automatic strategic commitment.
Fail closed when
No clear owner, decision criterion, counterfactual, provenance, or stop condition.
UC-02Procurement

AI procurement and vendor authorization

Grant only scoped pilot permission after equal-budget tests, policy checks, and exit-readiness review.

Evidence burden
Comparable trials, policy and data boundary, security review, pilot certificate, exit plan.
Authority boundary
Procurement, security, legal, and business owners approve only the declared pilot scope.
Fail closed when
Unequal evaluation, hidden data dependency, unresolved policy breach, or no exit route.
UC-03Technology

Autonomous software delivery

Turn patches into test-backed, provenance-bound, rollback-ready release candidates.

Evidence burden
Change provenance, tests, reviewer trace, dependency record, release and rollback plan.
Authority boundary
Authorized human release owner approves deployment; the reference system never auto-deploys.
Fail closed when
Failing tests, secret exposure, missing provenance, unreviewed dependencies, or no rollback.
UC-04Security

Defensive cybersecurity remediation

Convert findings into secret-safe, reviewable, minimal defensive change.

Evidence burden
Finding proof, minimal patch, test evidence, secret-safe logs, review record, rollback.
Authority boundary
Security owner authorizes defensive deployment inside an explicit system boundary.
Fail closed when
Offensive scope, unverified finding, leaked secrets, excessive change, or absent rollback.
UC-05Science

Scientific discovery and experimental design

Prioritize falsifiable hypotheses, controls, assays, and evidence-producing experiments.

Evidence burden
Evidence graph, competing mechanisms, controls, assays, uncertainty, falsification plan, lineage.
Authority boundary
Principal investigator and applicable ethics or safety bodies authorize physical execution.
Fail closed when
No competing hypothesis, inadequate controls, non-falsifiable objective, or broken lineage.
UC-06Infrastructure

Critical infrastructure and energy resilience

Recommend bounded interventions with baseline fidelity, safety, pilot, stop, and rollback conditions.

Evidence burden
Baseline, instrumentation, sensitivity analysis, bounded pilot, stop rules, rollback plan.
Authority boundary
Designated operator and applicable safety or regulatory authority authorize field action.
Fail closed when
Weak baseline, unsafe envelope, missing instrumentation, no stop rule, or irreversible pilot.
UC-07Public

Policy and regulatory mission operations

Preserve primary law, plurality, distributional effects, uncertainty, and institutional authority.

Evidence burden
Primary sources, stakeholder plurality, impact analysis, dissent record, dependencies, reversal conditions.
Authority boundary
Only the legally empowered public institution may decide or implement policy.
Fail closed when
Stale law, single-viewpoint analysis, hidden distributional effects, or no dissent pathway.
UC-08Capital

M&A, partnership, and investment diligence

Bind strategic claims to dependencies, counterfactuals, integration conditions, and value realization.

Evidence burden
Claim-dependency matrix, downside cases, counterfactuals, integration plan, value-realization tests.
Authority boundary
Investment committee, board, or delegated owner records the transaction decision.
Fail closed when
Unbound claims, absent downside case, no integration conditions, or unsupported value thesis.
UC-09Economy

Proof-gated agentic work markets

Commission, execute, validate, challenge, and prepare settlement without unearned authority.

Evidence burden
Identity, mission charter, coalition record, receipts, validator opinions, dissent, challenge, settlement intent.
Authority boundary
Human settlement review remains mandatory; no live value moves in the reference release.
Fail closed when
Self-validation, hidden coalition, missing challenge rights, unresolved dissent, or automatic settlement.
UC-10Publication

Autonomous institutional publication

Generate public artifacts from proof-aligned source through deterministic QA and human-review pull requests.

Evidence burden
Source lineage, deterministic build, accessibility, link and visual checks, claim review, PR evidence.
Authority boundary
A human reviewer merges and deploys; generated material cannot publish itself.
Fail closed when
Source drift, failed QA, inaccessible output, unsafe claims, or absent human review.
