# Safe Claims and Token Boundary

## Approved

AGIALPHA is used as a utility coordination token for proof-based AI workflow work.

Sponsors use AGIALPHA to post missions.
Builders use AGIALPHA to claim work and submit proof.
Reviewers use AGIALPHA to validate proof.
Approved proof creates Proof Cards, credentials, reputation, and access to better opportunities.

## Prohibited

- investment
- profit
- yield
- revenue share
- passive income
- price target
- guaranteed resale value
- guaranteed non-security
- audited unless final audit exists
- legal/tax approved unless memos exist
- autonomous AGI enterprise claims without governance/proof boundaries
