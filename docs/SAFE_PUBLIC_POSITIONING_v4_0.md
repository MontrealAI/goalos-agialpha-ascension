## Best line

GoalOS decides what may evolve. AGIALPHA coordinates proof-settled work. Evidence Dockets make claims auditable.

GoalOS turns institutional aims into signed commitments. Agents execute bounded work off-chain. ProofBundles, evaluations, replay records, selection certificates, evidence dockets and chronicles make the work auditable. AGIALPHA coordinates staking, settlement, proof-work incentives and accountability.
