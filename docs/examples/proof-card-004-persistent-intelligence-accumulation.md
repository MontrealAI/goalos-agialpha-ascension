# Proof Card 004 — Persistent Goal-Seeking Intelligence Accumulation Engine

> GoalOS AGIALPHA Ascension is an experimental framework for a persistent, goal-seeking, self-improving intelligence system that accumulates capabilities, evidence, and economic value over time.

## Plain-English meaning

Proof Cards 001-003 show useful proof missions. Proof Card 004 shows the accumulation layer: the system can remember what proved itself, reuse accepted artifacts, and make future work better without allowing unproven changes to propagate.

## Why this matters

Most AI work is episodic. A prompt is used, a response is produced, and the lesson is often lost. GoalOS AGIALPHA Ascension changes that pattern by turning completed work into a candidate artifact, then requiring evidence, review, and Selection Gate approval before that artifact can shape future workflows.

## Where AGIALPHA becomes useful

AGIALPHA coordinates the proof-work economy: mission posting, builder claim bonds, proof submission bonds, reviewer bonds, proof-card actions, credential actions, reward settlement, and reputation-linked access.

## Smart-contract / registry route

1. AEPGoalOSCommitRegistry — records the persistent aim.
2. AEPRunCommitmentRegistry — commits the route, tools, context roots, evaluators, and rollback duties.
3. JobRegistry — posts the proof mission.
4. JobClaimBondManager — locks builder claim bond.
5. ProofSubmissionRegistry — records proof hash and proof-card hash.
6. ReviewerBondRegistry — bonds reviewers and records validation.
7. ProofCardRegistry — publishes the public-safe proof card.
8. ProofCredentialRegistry — issues non-transferable credential.
9. ReputationRegistry — updates proof-backed reputation.
10. AEPEvidenceDocketRegistry — anchors the proof room.
11. AEPSelectionGate — grants or denies scoped upgrade rights.
12. AEPChronicleRegistry — records durable institutional memory.

## Sovereign RSI loop

Observe weakness -> improve artifact -> prove change -> review evidence -> select -> grant scoped upgrade rights -> improve future work -> chronicle the result.

## What accumulates

- Capabilities: workflows, evidence templates, reviewer rubrics, procurement responses, support policies, rollback plans, and proof-mission plans.
- Evidence: ProofBundles, EvalAttestations, Evidence Dockets, reviewer decisions, challenge records, replay logs, and Chronicle entries.
- Economic value: measurable workflow value, reduced repeated work, sponsor demand, buyer trust, and better routing. This is not a token-price or investment-return claim.

## Claim boundary

This is an illustrative proof card until a live Evidence Docket is completed. It does not claim achieved AGI, achieved ASI, guaranteed ROI, legal/tax/security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or investment return.
