# Proof Card 002 - Sovereign Procurement Trust Room

Help serious buyers understand what can be trusted, audited, and safely shared.

## Mission

Build a procurement trust room that explains the product, proof flow, claim boundaries, on-chain/off-chain split, review controls, and evidence requirements.

## Problem

Enterprise and sovereign buyers do not buy vague AI claims. They need evidence, boundaries, review logic, and a clear path to due diligence.

## AGIALPHA route

AGIALPHA coordinates mission budgets, bonds, proof submission, review, proof cards, credentials, reputation, settlement, treasury routing, and Chronicle memory.

## RSI logic

Recursive Self-Improvement is implemented as proof-backed upgrade rights: only reviewed, replayable, selected artifacts may influence future work.

## Claim boundary

Experimental proof-card template; not a claim of achieved AGI, financial return, legal/tax/security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
