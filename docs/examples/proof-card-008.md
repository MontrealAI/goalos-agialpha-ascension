# Proof Card 008 - Sovereign Corporate Capability Foundry

Coordinate portfolios of proof missions across high-value sovereign corporate domains.

## Mission

Turn the autoresearch reactor into a capability foundry that manages domain portfolios, benchmarks, experiments, and reinvestment decisions.

## Problem

A single successful mission is not enough. Institutions need a portfolio system that allocates proof work across procurement, finance, compliance, security, operations, compute, and R&D.

## AGIALPHA route

AGIALPHA coordinates mission budgets, bonds, proof submission, review, proof cards, credentials, reputation, settlement, treasury routing, and Chronicle memory.

## RSI logic

Recursive Self-Improvement is implemented as proof-backed upgrade rights: only reviewed, replayable, selected artifacts may influence future work.

## Claim boundary

Experimental proof-card template; not a claim of achieved AGI, financial return, legal/tax/security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
