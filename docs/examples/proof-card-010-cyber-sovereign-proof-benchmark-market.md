# Proof Card 010 — Cyber-Sovereign Proof Benchmark Market

**Domain:** defensive cyber benchmarks

**Status:** Experimental public proof-card dossier / evidence-docket template

## Plain-English thesis

Cyber-sovereign work becomes a replayable benchmark market with independent reproduction receipts.

## Mission

Create a cyber-sovereign proof benchmark market: define authorized defensive tasks, run parallel specialist-agent missions, validate outputs through evaluator harnesses, require red-team challenge, collect independent reproduction receipts, settle through AGIALPHA, and chronicle selected artifacts.

## Why it matters

This card is the strongest execution-moat card because it converts proof architecture into a market-readable standard for replayable results.

## AGIALPHA usage

AGIALPHA coordinates benchmark mission budgets, builder claims, proof submissions, reviewer/red-team bonds, evaluator incentives, proof-card actions, settlement, and reputation-weighted routing.

## RSI logic

Proof benchmark RSI means replayed, validated, and selected results can influence future missions; unreproduced results cannot.

## Claim boundary

This proof card does not claim achieved AGI, superintelligence, guaranteed return, legal approval, tax approval, security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
