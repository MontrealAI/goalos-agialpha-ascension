# Proof Card 003 - Sovereign AI Procurement Control Tower

Coordinate procurement proof, risk, review, and rollout decisions across a serious buyer workflow.

## Mission

Create a procurement control tower: map buyer risk, prepare evidence, assign specialist cells, validate proof, produce a public-safe card, and route accepted artifacts to future trust-room work.

## Problem

A sovereign or enterprise buyer needs to decide whether an AI workflow system can be trusted, governed, audited, rolled back, and improved safely.

## AGIALPHA route

AGIALPHA coordinates mission budgets, bonds, proof submission, review, proof cards, credentials, reputation, settlement, treasury routing, and Chronicle memory.

## RSI logic

Recursive Self-Improvement is implemented as proof-backed upgrade rights: only reviewed, replayable, selected artifacts may influence future work.

## Claim boundary

Experimental proof-card template; not a claim of achieved AGI, financial return, legal/tax/security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
