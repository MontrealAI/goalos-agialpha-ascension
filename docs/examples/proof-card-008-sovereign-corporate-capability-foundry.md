# Proof Card 008 - Sovereign Corporate Capability Foundry

**Thesis:** A sovereign enterprise can allocate proof missions across domains to manufacture reusable capability, not just isolated task outputs.

## Problem
Corporate AI programs often create disconnected experiments that fail to become durable capability across departments.

## Proof mission
Build a portfolio foundry that turns domain weaknesses into proof missions, accepted artifacts, credentials, reputation, and capability budgets.

## AGIALPHA use
