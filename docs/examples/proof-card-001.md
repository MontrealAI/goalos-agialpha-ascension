# Proof Card 001 - Buyer Rescue Workflow

Turn a buyer support breakdown into a proof-backed workflow upgrade.

## Mission

Convert the support incident into a GoalOS proof mission: classify the issue, draft a clear response, score the response, improve the workflow, record a public-safe Proof Card, and preserve private buyer data off-chain.

## Problem

A buyer cannot access a download or paid resource. A one-off AI reply might be helpful, but the institution needs a reusable workflow that prevents repeat confusion, handles escalation, and leaves evidence.

## AGIALPHA route

AGIALPHA coordinates mission budgets, bonds, proof submission, review, proof cards, credentials, reputation, settlement, treasury routing, and Chronicle memory.

## RSI logic

Recursive Self-Improvement is implemented as proof-backed upgrade rights: only reviewed, replayable, selected artifacts may influence future work.

## Claim boundary

Experimental proof-card template; not a claim of achieved AGI, financial return, legal/tax/security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
