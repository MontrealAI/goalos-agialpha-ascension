# Proof Card 003 — Sovereign AI Procurement Control Tower

**Domain:** AI procurement / governance

**Status:** Experimental public proof-card dossier / evidence-docket template

## Plain-English thesis

A due-diligence question becomes a governed procurement control tower.

## Mission

Build a procurement control tower that decomposes AI-adoption questions into proof missions, routes them to specialist agents, validates evidence, and produces a public-safe decision dossier.

## Why it matters

This shifts GoalOS from a product into a decision infrastructure layer for AI adoption. It becomes the control plane for institutional AI procurement.

## AGIALPHA usage

AGIALPHA funds and coordinates the procurement proof mission portfolio, including builder, reviewer, red-team, proof-card, credential, and reputation actions.

## RSI logic

Procurement criteria and rubrics evolve only when validated decisions improve future evaluations without weakening risk controls.

## Claim boundary

This proof card does not claim achieved AGI, superintelligence, guaranteed return, legal approval, tax approval, security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
