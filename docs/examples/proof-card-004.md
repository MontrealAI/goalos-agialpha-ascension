# Proof Card 004 - Persistent Goal-Seeking Intelligence Accumulation Engine

Show how the system accumulates capability, evidence, and economic value over time.

## Mission

Define a persistent GoalOS loop where missions produce artifacts, artifacts produce evidence, evidence produces selection, and selected artifacts improve future work.

## Problem

One-off automations do not create durable institutional intelligence unless the system remembers what worked, where, why, and under what constraints.

## AGIALPHA route

AGIALPHA coordinates mission budgets, bonds, proof submission, review, proof cards, credentials, reputation, settlement, treasury routing, and Chronicle memory.

## RSI logic

Recursive Self-Improvement is implemented as proof-backed upgrade rights: only reviewed, replayable, selected artifacts may influence future work.

## Claim boundary

Experimental proof-card template; not a claim of achieved AGI, financial return, legal/tax/security approval, Ethereum mainnet deployment, or Kardashev Type II achievement.
