# Auditor Handoff v4.0

Audit the complete v4.0 suite, with special focus on:

1. Existing AGIALPHA token integration.
2. No new AGIALPHA deployment or minting on mainnet.
3. Proof-work settlement flows.
4. Rejected/expired job refunds and bond settlement.
5. ProofBundle and replay registry assumptions.
6. Commit-reveal validation correctness.
7. Evaluator staking and slashing court authority.
8. Reward vault custody and operator role risks.
9. Claim-boundary and conformance records.
10. Mainnet deployment gates and manifests.
11. AccessControl privilege concentration.
12. Pause and incident response.

## Out of scope unless separately engaged

- legal/security classification of AGIALPHA;
- tax treatment;
- investment/promoter compliance;
- economic value of AGIALPHA;
- off-chain AI workflow correctness;
- private evidence-store security.

## Required auditor output

- findings;
- severity;
- remediation status;
- retest evidence;
- residual risks;
- mainnet recommendation.
