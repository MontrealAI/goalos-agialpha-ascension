Evidence Docket

The proof page people can share.

A public-safe Evidence Docket shows what you tried, what happened, what worked, what failed, and what should happen next.

Docket sections

SectionPurpose
ClaimWhat did you prove?
BaselineWhere did you start?
ActionsWhat did you do?
EvidenceWhat files, screenshots, links, replies, results exist?
CostTime, money, and energy spent.
Risk boundaryWhat should not be claimed?
Selection decisionPromote, retry, reject, or rollback.
Reusable capabilityWhat will you use again?
Next testWhat should happen next?
