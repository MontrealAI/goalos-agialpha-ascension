Roadmap

From one useful proof to compounding capability.

The path stays ambitious but bounded: prove small wins, make them reusable, scale only what survives evidence, and reinvest verified value into stronger capability.

Milestones

1

First useful mission

A regular person completes one proof-backed task.

2

Proof Card

A public-safe proof summary explains the result and boundary.

3

Evidence Docket

The underlying proof is inspectable without exposing private data.

4

Mission market

Sponsors, builders, reviewers, and credentials coordinate through AGIALPHA.

5

Institutional adoption

Businesses and communities reuse verified workflows.

6

Capability grid

Verified value funds stronger agents, tools, audits, and infrastructure.

Kardashev-aligned horizon

Long-term ambition: verified intelligence creates such useful capability that it funds stronger infrastructure, compute, science, and energy-relevant experimentation. Current boundary: this is an evidence-gated path, not a claim of achieved superintelligence or civilization-scale status.
