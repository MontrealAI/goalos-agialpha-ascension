Proof Gradient control plane

RSI means proof-backed upgrade rights.

Recursive Self-Improvement is treated as a governance route: an artifact may influence future work only after evidence, evaluation, review, challenge, canary, monitoring, and rollback readiness. It does not claim achieved AGI, achieved ASI, or superintelligence achieved.

v85 final operating brief

Deterministic RSI governance

Recursive self-improvement earns authority only through proof.

GoalOS treats this surface as an operating instrument rather than a marketing page. The page should let a nontechnical user understand the decision, while giving a builder or reviewer enough structure to inspect what the system would execute, what evidence it must produce, and what would stop the workflow. The stable unit is not a prompt or an answer; it is a bounded mission whose artifacts can be checked, challenged, replayed, and remembered.

The institutional advantage comes from separation of concerns. Models generate and reason; agents use tools; Mission OS defines the objective and permissions; the Evidence Docket binds claims to sources, traces, costs, risks, and tests; reviewers decide whether the result can influence future work; Chronicle memory stores what should be reused. Where economic settlement is needed, $AGIALPHA is introduced only after proof exists, as utility for escrow, bonds, validator incentives, α‑Work Units, disputes, and capacity allocation.

Operator checklist

The objective, authority, and success criteria are explicit.
The proof object can be inspected by someone other than the generating agent.
The risk, privacy, legal, security, and rollback boundaries are recorded.
The result has a decision state: accept, repair, reject, canary, or keep pending.

Evidence rule

Architecture and simulations can justify a design claim. Strong operational claims require real tasks, baselines, complete Evidence Dockets, replay, cost and risk ledgers, validator reports, delayed outcomes, and independent review. The website must always make the current evidence level visible.
